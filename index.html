Lash Services

All lash services are done with premium mink lashes

Classic

This is the most traditional form of eyelash extensions. We're talking a 1:1 ratio of one extension applied to one natural lash. This means we can only apply as many extensions as you have natural lashes. So, if you have sparse lashes and wish to fill out your lash line, classic eyelash extensions won't be the most suitable fit for you.

Volume

A new and innovative advancement in eyelash extension application, Volume Extensions. This method involves applying 2-6 very fine lashes (2D - 6D) to each one natural lash, that means 200-500 extensions per eye! This is the perfect solution for those craving va-va volume!

Mega Volume

Volume to the max! This method involves applying 8-15 even finer lashes to each one natural lash creating a black out lash line!
